# NoteX Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 10, 2026**

NoteX is built around the principle that your notes are yours. This policy explains what data the app touches and what it doesn't.

## What we don't collect

NoteX does not run any server. We don't have a database of your notes. We don't track your usage, IP address, location, contacts, or any analytics. We don't use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

## How your notes are stored

Notes you write in NoteX are stored on your device and synced through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit private database. This means:

- Notes are stored in your iCloud, not ours.
- Only you, signed into your Apple ID, can read them.
- Apple encrypts the data in transit and at rest.
- If you sign out of iCloud, the cloud copy is removed by Apple.

If iCloud is unavailable or you've disabled it, NoteX falls back to storing notes locally on the current device only.

## AI features (Brian)

Brian is the in-app AI. Brian runs entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence (Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework). Your notes, questions, and AI responses never leave your device. We don't see them. Apple doesn't see them.

This applies to:

- Asking Brian questions about your notes
- Generating connections between notes
- Summarizing notebooks
- Creating quizzes and flashcards

## Voice transcription

Voice dictation uses Apple's on-device Speech Recognition. The app requires the on-device variant — your voice audio is never sent to Apple's servers or any third party.

## Photo OCR

When you scan a photo to extract text, the photo and the recognized text are processed locally using Apple's Vision framework. The photo and the resulting text never leave your device.

## Subscriptions

In-app subscriptions are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive your payment information. Apple shares with us only whether you have an active subscription — no personal details.

To manage or cancel: Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your Apple device.

## Children

NoteX is intended for users 13 and older. We don't knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. If you believe a child has used the app, there is no data for us to remove because we don't have any.

## Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy in a way that meaningfully affects your data, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Because we don't have your email or any contact information, we cannot notify you directly.
